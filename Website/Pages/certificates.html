Navachar Certificate


The Navachar Certificate is reserved for newcomers who quickly rise to the challenge, contributing significantly to our team's goals and vision. It recognizes their exceptional dedication and impact, setting them apart as trailblazers in our community.

Vikaaskarta Certificate


The Vikaaskarta Certificate is the pinnacle of recognition within our team, awarded to individuals who exemplify unwavering dedication, innovation, and leadership. It signifies a commitment to fostering growth and excellence, acknowledging those who consistently contribute to our collective success.

NAZI Certificate
This certificate is named after the acronym "NAZI," representing the organization's values and mission. "Nirmaan Aadharit Zimmedar India" translates to "Responsible Development-Based India," signifying the recipients' commitment to the organization's goals and their responsible approach to their work.

Jaanleva Certificate


The term "Jaanleva" translates to "deadly" or "fatal" in Hindi. In this context, it symbolizes the exceptional dedication, skill, and impact that recipients of this certificate bring to their roles. Their contributions are so significant and transformative that they are deemed "Jaanleva" or highly impactful.

Inhumane Mahan Certificate


The name "Inhumane Mahan" reflects the extraordinary dedication, achievement, and innovation exemplified by recipients of this certificate. The term "Inhumane" suggests a level of dedication that transcends typical human limitations, while "Mahan" means "great" or "magnificent" in Hindi, emphasizing the exceptional nature of the recognition.
